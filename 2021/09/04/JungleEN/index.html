Bright colors and psychedelic lines emphasize the strength of natural forces in his works, yet the human emotions and energy are surging in the dark. He selects a small section of each motif from his daily accumulated archive, then reorganizes them to let these rich and figurative elements reflect each other, intrinsically connecting the universal but indescribable life experience of people.
